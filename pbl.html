What is PBL?

Project-Based Learning (PBL) is a student-centered pedagogy where learners explore real-world problems, working over time to collaborate, design solutions, and present outcomes.

At SGVP, PBL is integrated through seminars, group projects, field visits, and cooperative learning, fostering critical thinking and communication skills.
